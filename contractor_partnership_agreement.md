## RECITALS

WHEREAS, MBRACE operates an intelligence platform that helps property owners understand heat pump incentive eligibility in Maryland;

WHEREAS, Contractor is a licensed HVAC contractor with experience installing heat pump systems and participating in EmPOWER Maryland rebate programs;

WHEREAS, the parties wish to establish a referral relationship whereby MBRACE may refer qualified leads to Contractor, and Contractor may refer customers to MBRACE's calculator tool;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements set forth herein, the parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. DEFINITIONS

**1.1 "Lead"** means a property owner who has completed MBRACE's incentive calculator, expressed interest in heat pump installation, and been referred to Contractor by MBRACE.

**1.2 "Qualified Lead"** means a Lead who (a) owns or controls a property in Contractor's Service Territory, (b) has a functioning contact method, and (c) has indicated intent to consider heat pump installation within 12 months.

**1.3 "Closed Project"** means a heat pump installation project that (a) originated from a Lead referred by MBRACE, (b) was installed by Contractor, and (c) has been paid in full by the property owner.

**1.4 "Service Territory"** means the geographic area(s) in which Contractor is authorized to receive referrals, as specified in Exhibit A.

**1.5 "EmPOWER Participating Contractor"** means a contractor listed on an EmPOWER Maryland utility's approved contractor list for HVAC rebate programs.

---

## 2. CONTRACTOR REQUIREMENTS

**2.1 Licensing.** Contractor represents and warrants that it holds a valid Maryland HVACR license and all other licenses required to perform heat pump installations in its Service Territory.

**2.2 Insurance.** Contractor shall maintain throughout the term of this Agreement:
- General liability insurance: minimum $1,000,000 per occurrence
- Workers' compensation insurance: as required by Maryland law
- Automobile liability insurance: minimum $500,000 combined single limit

Contractor shall provide certificates of insurance upon request.

**2.3 EmPOWER Participation.** Contractor represents that it is currently an EmPOWER Participating Contractor with at least one Maryland utility, OR that it has applied for such status and will notify MBRACE upon approval.

**2.4 Experience.** Contractor represents that it has completed at least [10] heat pump installations in the past 12 months and has experience with cold-climate heat pump systems.

**2.5 Quality Standards.** Contractor agrees to:
- Perform proper load calculations (Manual J/S/D or equivalent) for all installations
- Install only AHRI-matched systems with appropriate efficiency ratings
- Follow manufacturer installation guidelines
- Provide required documentation for rebate processing
- Maintain professional communication with referred Leads

---

## 3. MBRACE OBLIGATIONS

**3.1 Lead Referral.** MBRACE will refer Qualified Leads to Contractor based on:
- Geographic match (property in Contractor's Service Territory)
- Contractor capacity and availability
- Lead preferences (if expressed)

**3.2 Lead Information.** For each referral, MBRACE will provide:
- Property owner contact information (name, phone, email)
- Property address
- Building type and current heating system
- Incentive eligibility summary
- Any notes from lead qualification

**3.3 Exclusivity.** Unless otherwise agreed in Exhibit A, referrals are non-exclusive. MBRACE may refer the same Lead to multiple contractors, and Contractor may receive leads from other sources.

**3.4 No Guarantee.** MBRACE does not guarantee any minimum number of referrals, lead quality, or conversion rates. Referral volume depends on market conditions and lead flow.

---

## 4. REFERRAL PROCESS

**4.1 Referral Delivery.** MBRACE will deliver Lead referrals via [email / SMS / CRM portal] to Contractor's designated contact.

**4.2 Response Time.** Contractor agrees to contact each referred Lead within [24-48] hours of receiving the referral.

**4.3 Outcome Reporting.** Contractor agrees to report the outcome of each referral within [30] days, including:
- Whether contact was made
- Whether a quote was provided
- Whether the project closed
- Project value (if closed)

**4.4 Lead Ownership.** Leads referred by MBRACE remain the property of the Lead (i.e., the property owner). Neither MBRACE nor Contractor "owns" the Lead. If a Lead does not respond to Contractor, Contractor shall not continue contact attempts beyond [3] attempts without the Lead's consent.

**5.1 Referral Fee Structure.** [SELECT ONE OPTION]

**Option A: Per-Lead Fee**

**Option B: Per-Closed-Project Fee**

**5.3 Reporting & Audit.** MBRACE reserves the right to request documentation verifying Closed Projects for referrals within the past [12] months.

## 7. CONDUCT & COMPLIANCE

**7.1 Professional Conduct.** Contractor shall conduct all interactions with referred Leads in a professional manner consistent with industry standards.

**7.2 No High-Pressure Sales.** Contractor shall not engage in high-pressure sales tactics, misrepresent product capabilities, or make promises regarding incentive amounts that are not supported by program guidelines.

**7.3 Compliance.** Contractor shall comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws, including but not limited to:
- Maryland HVACR licensing requirements
- EmPOWER program rules and requirements
- Consumer protection laws
- Privacy and data protection laws

**7.4 Incentive Accuracy.** Contractor shall not misrepresent incentive amounts to Leads. Contractor understands that MBRACE's calculator provides estimates, and actual rebate amounts depend on program availability, equipment selection, and application outcomes.

---

## 8. DATA & PRIVACY

**8.1 Lead Data.** Contractor shall use Lead information only for the purpose of providing quotes and services related to heat pump installation. Contractor shall not sell, share, or use Lead data for unrelated marketing purposes.

**8.2 Data Protection.** Contractor shall implement reasonable security measures to protect Lead data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

**8.3 Retention.** Upon termination of this Agreement, Contractor may retain Lead data only for Leads with whom Contractor has an ongoing customer relationship.

---

## 9. TERM & TERMINATION

**9.1 Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for [12] months ("Initial Term"), unless terminated earlier pursuant to this Section. After the Initial Term, this Agreement shall renew automatically for successive [12]-month periods unless either party provides [30] days' written notice of non-renewal.

**9.2 Termination for Convenience.** Either party may terminate this Agreement for any reason upon [30] days' written notice to the other party.

**9.3 Termination for Cause.** Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party:
- Breaches any material term of this Agreement and fails to cure within [15] days of notice
- Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy
- Engages in illegal or unethical conduct

**9.4 Effect of Termination.** Upon termination:
- Contractor shall pay any outstanding referral fees within [15] days
- Contractor shall cease using MBRACE's name and materials
- Provisions relating to confidentiality and data protection shall survive
