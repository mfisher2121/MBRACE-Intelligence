# MBRACE Google Ads Campaign Structure
## Maryland Heat Pump Incentive Calculator — Paid Search Strategy

---

## Campaign Overview

**Objective:** Drive calculator submissions from property owners actively researching heat pump incentives, rebates, or Maryland electrification requirements.

**Geographic Targeting:** Maryland + DC metro (include NoVA for spillover)

**Budget Recommendation:** $50-100/day to start, scale based on CPA

**Target CPA:** $15-40 per calculator submission (varies by segment)

## Optimization Checklist

### Week 1
- [ ] Launch all campaigns
- [ ] Verify conversion tracking fires correctly
- [ ] Check search terms report daily
- [ ] Add negative keywords as needed

### Week 2
- [ ] Review CPA by ad group
- [ ] Pause underperforming keywords (high spend, no conversions)
- [ ] Increase bids on converting keywords
- [ ] Test new ad copy variants

### Week 3
- [ ] Analyze device performance (mobile vs desktop)
- [ ] Review time-of-day performance
- [ ] Adjust bid modifiers
- [ ] Expand keyword list based on search terms

### Month 2+
- [ ] A/B test landing pages
- [ ] Test audience expansion
- [ ] Consider Display campaigns for awareness
- [ ] Review competitor ads for new angles
