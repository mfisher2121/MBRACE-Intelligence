Do heat pumps work in Maryland winters?

Yes. Modern cold-climate heat pumps work efficiently down to -15¬∞F. Maryland rarely sees temperatures that low. The "heat pumps don't work in cold weather" myth is based on 1980s technology.

What if my income is too high for the big rebates?

You still qualify for EmPOWER utility rebates ($2,000-$8,000) plus the federal 25C tax credit ($2,000). Total: $4,000-$10,000 even for higher earners.

Why is Maryland pushing heat pumps?

Maryland signed the 2024 Zero-Emission Buildings MOU requiring 65% heat pump market share by 2030. The state is using rebates now to make the transition affordable before mandates kick in.
